ABOUT NCC IIT ROORKEE

The National Cadet Corps (NCC) is a youth development movement. It has enormous potential for nation building. The NCC provides opportunities to the youth of the country for their all-round development with a sense of Duty, Commitment, Dedication, Discipline and Moral Values so that they become able leaders and useful citizens. The NCC provides exposure to the cadets in a wide range of activities, with a distinct emphasis on Social Services, Discipline and Adventure Training.

The National Cadet Corps, IIT Roorkee under the unit 3 UK Composite Technical Regiment National Cadet Corps is a student unit of the Indian Army which aims to develop a sense of discipline, rigour and unity in the cadets. It provides a platform to the students of the college to develop their personality by engaging themselves in various events organised by it. The Guard of Honour, Indoor and outdoor sports events, firing practice and Training camps aim at inculcating the values of team-work and discipline in the cadets along with an exposure to life in the army. At the same me, working for the society, the NCC also conducts plantation and cleanliness drives, awareness campaigns and activities for social causes to instil the spirit of service in the young cadets. Currently, the NCC has 420 acre members who are continuously guided by the Commanding Officer of the unit and are working towards self-development and nation-building. ‘UNITY AND DISCIPLINE’, the motto of the NCC precisely highlights the aim and culture of the unit.

ACTIVITIES

General

  • Parades are regularly held to train the cadets in foot drill and command, weapon training, field craft, civil defence, map reading etc.
  • Guard of Honour is given on 26th January and 15th August to commemorate the Republic Day and Independence Day of India.

Camp

  • An Annual Training Camp is held to further enhance and inculcate a feeling of independence in the cadets.
  • Extensive knowledge about devices used in the Army like mines, bridges, nuclear and biological weapons etc. is imparted.
  • Self-defence training is also given
  • Rigorous physical training is provided in the camp with a view to increasing the physical endurance of the cadets.
  • Military training with rifles is also provided.
  • Cultural programmes are also held with a view to improving the cultural and social outlook of the cadets.

Social

  • Various social activities are also organized to encourage the feeling of belonging to the society like awareness campaigns, nukkad-nataks and cycle rallies.
  • Under 'Swach Bharat Mission,' Swachta Pakhwada event, various activities like clean village drives, cycle rallies, awareness campaigns are organized.

Cultural

  • Various Cultural activities like Debates, Nukkad Natak, Article Writing Competitions, Painting competitions, Poster Making competitions are conducted among the cadets.
  • Cadets get the opportunity to participate and represent NCC IIT Roorkee in national level cultural events like Ek Bharat Shrestha Bharat.

Sports & Other Activities

  • Drills, Marathons, Fitness sessions, Sports Day, etc. are organized.
  • Both Indoor & Outdoor Sports events are conducted for the cadets
  • Quizzes, Visits to BEG IIT Roorkee, Webinars & Seminars by Indian Army Officers, and many other eminent personalities inspire & inform the cadets about The Great Indian Armed Forces.
